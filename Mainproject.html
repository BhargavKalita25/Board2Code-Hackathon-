Tiki Topple - Playable Hackathon Game

Local multiplayer strategy game. On each turn, a player may either move the top 1-3 tokens forward by 1 step or reorder the top 2-3 tokens to control the stack.

Rules used here: one action per turn, only top tokens can be touched, and scoring is based on final ranking across all tokens. Tokens belong to players, so you want your own colors to finish higher.
